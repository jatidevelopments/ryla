> **Created**: January 2026  
> **Status**: Strategic Analysis & Recommendations  
> **Goal**: Position RYLA pricing slightly below competitors while maintaining healthy margins

---

## Executive Summary

**Current Position**: RYLA is competitive on per-image costs ($0.005-0.015 vs $0.063 market average) but entry tier pricing ($29/mo) is above market average ($10.83/mo).

**Strategy**: Adjust pricing to be **5-15% cheaper** than direct competitors while maintaining 4-5x margins on self-hosted features.

**Target Outcome**: 
- Lower barrier to entry (improve conversion)
- Maintain profitability (4-5x margins)
- Competitive positioning (slightly cheaper than alternatives)
- **Superior buying power** (10-30x more images than competitors)

## Recommended Pricing Strategy

### Goal: Be 2-3x Better Value Than Competitors (Not 15-30x)

**Strategy Principles:**
1. **Entry tier**: Match or beat budget leaders ($9-14/mo range)
2. **Pro tier**: Maintain competitive position ($49/mo is good)
3. **Unlimited tier**: Keep at $99/mo (already competitive)
4. **Annual discount**: Increase to 40-50% (match market standard)
5. **Per-image costs**: **ADJUST** to be 2-3x better (not 15-30x better)
6. **Credit costs**: Increase feature costs to align with market positioning
