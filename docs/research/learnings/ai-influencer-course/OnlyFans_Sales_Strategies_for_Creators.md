## 8\. Common Mistakes & How to Avoid Them

### Mistake 1: Hard Selling Too Early

Problem: "Hi, want to buy my $50 video pack?" Fix: Build rapport first â€“ 2-3 positive exchanges before offering

### Mistake 2: Inconsistent Voice

Problem: Switching between flirty, professional, casual tones Fix: Create 1 persona voice guide (e.g., "Always playful, confident, emoji-heavy")

### Mistake 3: Slow Response Times

Problem: Fans wait 24+ hours for replies Fix: Set notifications to "always" â€“ respond within 1 hour (80% of sales happen in first conversation)

### Mistake 4: No Follow-Up

Problem: Fans show interest but never buy Fix: Automated 3-day follow-up sequence for warm leads

### Mistake 5: Over-Discounting

Problem: Everything 50% off erodes perceived value Fix: Limit discounts to 20-30%, use scarcity ("First 5 only!")

### Mistake 6: Ignoring VIP Fans

Problem: Treat all fans the same, top spenders feel unappreciated Fix: Personal weekly check-ins, exclusive previews for $100+ spenders
