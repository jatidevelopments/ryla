## Summary

Identifies three main reasons why AI influencers look fake/plastic and how to fix them:

1. **Using Wrong Tools**: Points-based sites (Picasso, OpenArt) vs credit-based (fal.ai, Wavespeed)
2. **Bad Prompting**: Basic prompts vs advanced prompts with realism keywords
3. **Old Models/Bad Workflows**: Using outdated models or non-optimized workflows

Solutions:
- Use fal.ai or Wavespeed (real pricing, better quality)
- Use advanced prompts (amateur photo, cell phone quality)
- Use modern models with realism LoRAs (smartphone snapshot photo reality)
