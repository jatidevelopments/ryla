## Summary

Marketing basics for AI influencers, focusing on the conversion funnel: Awareness → Interest → Desire → Purchase. Emphasizes that increasing awareness (reach) is more important than optimizing conversion rate.

Key strategies:
1. **Awareness**: Organic traffic, paid traffic, dating apps, chatting
2. **Interest/Desire**: Psychology tactics, provocative content, unique offers
3. **Purchase**: Irresistible offers, perceived uniqueness

Key insight: "If you increase reach from 1,000 to 1 million, you'll make money even with 0.5% conversion rate."
