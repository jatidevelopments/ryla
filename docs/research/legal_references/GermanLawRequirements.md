# German Legal Requirements for AI Platforms (2026)

## 1. Impressum (Imprint) - § 5 DDG

Mandatory for any commercial website in Germany.

- **Name and Address**: Full name of operator and legal entity, physical address (not a P.O. box).
- **Contact**: Email and Phone number.
- **Represented by**: Managing directors (for legal entities like GmbH).
- **Registration**: Commercial register, register court, and registration number.
- **VAT ID**: USt-IdNr according to § 27a UStG (if applicable).
- **Economic ID**: W-IdNr (mandatory from Dec 2026).
- **Responsible for Content**: Name and address according to § 18 MStV (for editorial content).

## 2. Privacy Policy (Datenschutzerklärung) - DSGVO/GDPR

- **Responsible Person (Controller)**: Clear identification of the responsible entity.
- **Data Protection Officer (DPO)**: If required by law.
- **Legal Basis**: Art. 6 GDPR (e.g., Performance of contract, Legitimate interest, Consent).
- **AI Specific Processing**:
  - **Prompts and Uploads**: Processing of user input for generation.
  - **Biometric Data**: If facial recognition or similar is used, explicit consent (Art. 9 GDPR) and a Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) are required.
- **EU AI Act (starting August 2026)**:
  - **Labeling**: AI-generated content must be clearly marked as such.
  - **Deepfakes**: Specialized transparency requirements for AI-generated images of real persons.
- **Third-Country Transfers**: Clear information about data processing in the USA (e.g., AWS, OpenAI, Stripe) and legal guarantees (Standard Contractual Clauses).
- **User Rights**: Art. 15-21 GDPR (Access, Deletion, Portability, etc.).
- **Cookie Consent**: "Opt-in" required for non-essential cookies.

## 3. Terms of Service (AGB)

- **Scope**: Agreement and termination.
- **Description of Service**: AI image/video generation.
- **Ownership/IP**:
  - AI-generated content often lacks human authorship and may not be copyrightable under German law.
  - Granting commercial rights to the user is standard for paid tiers.
- **Acceptable Use**: Prohibiting illegal content, harassment, and deepfakes of real individuals without consent.
- **Liability**: Limitations compliant with German consumer law (BGB).
