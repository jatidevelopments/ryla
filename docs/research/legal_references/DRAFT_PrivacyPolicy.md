## 3. Data Collection and Purposes

### 3.1 Account Information

When you register, we collect your email address, name, and password hash.

- **Purpose**: To manage your user account and provide access to our services.
- **Legal Basis**: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (Contract performance).

### 3.2 AI Generation Inputs (Prompts & Uploads)

We process the text prompts and images you upload ("Inputs").

- **Purpose**: To generate the requested AI content.
- **Legal Basis**: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (Contract performance).
- **Sensitive Data**: If your inputs contain "special categories of personal data" (such as biometrics from faces), we process this based on your **explicit consent** (Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR).

### 3.3 AI-Generated Outputs

We store the images and videos generated by our AI system.

- **Purpose**: To provide the generated content to you and maintain your generation history.
- **Legal Basis**: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
- **Transparency (EU AI Act)**: In accordance with the EU AI Act, we ensure that AI-generated content is appropriately labeled as synthetic.

### 3.4 Payment Data

Payments are processed by third-party processors (e.g., Stripe). We do not store full credit card details on our servers.

- **Purpose**: Billing and subscription management.
- **Legal Basis**: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
