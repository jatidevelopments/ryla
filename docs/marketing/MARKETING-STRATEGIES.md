# RYLA Marketing Strategies

> **Last Updated**: January 2026  
> **Target**: US-based first-time AI creators (93% first-timers) with AI OnlyFans intent (39%)

---

## Overview

This document outlines core marketing strategies for RYLA, focusing on cost-effective acquisition channels that reach our target audience: first-time AI creators who want to monetize AI influencers, primarily for OnlyFans/Fanvue.

**Key Principles**:
- **Speed > Perfection**: MVP mindset - test fast, iterate on data
- **Target High-Intent Users**: Focus on users actively searching for solutions
- **Leverage Platform Authority**: Use established platforms for faster reach
- **Creator-Centric**: Speak to creators, not enterprises
- **NSFW-Friendly**: Target platforms that allow adult content discussion

## Strategy 1: Parasite Marketing (SEO Leverage)

### What It Is

Publish content on high-authority platforms (Medium, Reddit, Quora, YouTube, etc.) that rank well in search engines, then drive traffic to RYLA through strategic links and CTAs.

### Why It Works for RYLA

1. **Fast SEO Results**: Leverage existing domain authority instead of building from scratch
2. **Target High-Intent Searches**: Users searching "how to create AI OnlyFans" find our content
3. **Low Cost**: Free to publish, only time investment
4. **Trust Building**: Established platforms provide credibility
5. **NSFW-Friendly Platforms**: Reddit, Medium allow adult content discussion

### Target Platforms

| Platform | Authority | NSFW Allowed | Best For | Effort |
|----------|-----------|--------------|----------|--------|
| **Reddit** | High | ✅ Yes | Q&A, tutorials, case studies | Medium |
| **Medium** | Very High | ✅ Yes | Long-form guides, tutorials | High |
| **Quora** | High | ⚠️ Limited | Q&A, "how to" content | Low |
| **YouTube** | Very High | ✅ Yes | Video tutorials, demos | High |
| **LinkedIn** | High | ❌ No | Professional angle (agencies) | Medium |
| **Dev.to** | Medium | ⚠️ Limited | Technical tutorials | Low |
| **GitHub** | Very High | ✅ Yes | Open source, workflows | Medium |

## Strategy 2: Paid Social Advertising

### Target Platforms

**Primary**: Facebook/Instagram (82% of users from m.facebook.com)
- **Why**: Mobile-first, high-intent users, proven conversion
- **Targeting**: 25-40, US, interests in OnlyFans, side hustles, AI tools
- **Budget**: Start $50/day, scale to $200/day

**Secondary**: TikTok, Reddit Ads
- **Why**: Creator-focused, younger audience
- **Targeting**: Similar to Facebook
- **Budget**: $30/day test budget

### Ad Creative Strategy

**Messaging** (validated from funnel):
- **Primary**: "Create AI OnlyFans Model Without Showing Your Face"
- **Secondary**: "Make Money with AI Influencers - No Experience Needed"
- **Pain Point**: "Stop juggling 5+ tools - all-in-one AI influencer platform"

**Creative Types**:
1. **Before/After**: Show character creation process
2. **Testimonials**: "I made $X with RYLA" (when available)
3. **Demo Videos**: Quick walkthrough of character creation
4. **Problem/Solution**: "Tired of inconsistent AI faces? RYLA fixes it"

### Funnel Optimization

**Current Performance** (Ghost Test):
- Funnel entry: 36/64 (56%)
- Character creation: 19/36 (53%)
- Payment: 4/19 (19%) ⚠️ Bug affected

**Optimization Priorities**:
1. Fix payment bug
2. Add trust signals (testimonials, user count)
3. Clear value proposition ("Here's what you get")
4. Money-back guarantee
5. Familiar payment processor (Stripe)

### Budget Allocation

| Stage | Budget | Goal |
|-------|--------|------|
| Testing | $50/day | Find winning creative/audience |
| Scaling | $200/day | Scale winning ads |
| Optimization | $300/day | Maximize ROAS |
